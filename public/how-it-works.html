A connected ecosystem solving fragmentation in modern family healthcare.

1. The Problem Statement

Currently, the healthcare system is highly fragmented:

  • Medical records are scattered across different hospitals.
  • Doctors lack access to comprehensive family medical histories.
  • Hereditary diseases are often diagnosed too late because the familial link was never mathematically tracked.
  • Patients carry physical files that can be lost or damaged.

2. The MedMe Solution

MedMe creates a unified, QR-based identity platform that acts as a single source of truth.

  • Universal Patient QR Identity Cards
  • Centralized Lab Reports & Prescriptions
  • Rule-based Hereditary Risk Detection
  • Real-time Immutable Activity Logging

4. Intelligent Family Health Tracking

MedMe connects family members into a digital Health Tree. It actively monitors for patterns in diagnoses across generations.

⚠️ System Alert: Hereditary Risk Detected

Condition: Diabetes

Reason for Alert:

  • Grandfather was diagnosed with Diabetes.
  • Father was diagnosed with Diabetes.
Action Recommended:
The system automatically flags this during the doctor's scan, prompting early screening for the patient (e.g., HbA1c Lab Test) before severe symptoms appear.

5. Doctor & Patient Workflows

For Doctors:

  • Scan patients instantly without paperwork.
  • Access historical Lab Reports & Scans.
  • Manage upcoming appointments.

For Patients:

  • Carry only a QR code.
  • View immutable timeline of all visits.
  • Receive automated notifications.

6. Future Scope & Scalability

Because the MedMe ecosystem is built on a scalable REST API with JWT Auth and a structured SQL schema, it is ready to integrate with:

  • National Identity Systems.
  • Pharmacy API networks for direct prescription fulfillment.
  • Machine Learning modules for predictive analytics on the massive anonymized data sets currently viewed in the Analytics Dashboard.
